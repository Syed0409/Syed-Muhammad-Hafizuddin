My Achievements

  • My best academic performance was coming close to completing my bachelor's degree with a strong GPA. I was forced to pursue a part-time job while obtaining my university degree since my family was unable to help me financially. I learned from this to prioritise my time and keep my attention on my objectives. I'm happy of my accomplishment and believe the lessons I've gained will benefit me in the future as I develop my profession.

  • I maintained a perfect GPA throughout high school. In my three years of college, I was able to volunteer for groups since I realised there was more I could do with my time. I stayed a member of my college's events club after realising that planning and organising events is my area of interest. By the time I was in my last year, I had a GPA of 3.7 out of 4.0 and was no longer only a volunteer but also the director of the events department.


  • My first extracurricular accomplishment was winning a medal in the handball competitions I participated in while pursuing my degree, the Karisma and C-Games. I came in second in the UITM Shah Alam Karisma Competition and third at the UITM Machang C-Games. I'm also one of the top players in that match. My second extracurricular success was winning a 200 ringgit cash prize in a pubg competition at Uitm Machang.
